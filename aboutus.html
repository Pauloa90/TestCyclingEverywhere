About Us

Cycling Everywhere is the fastest-growing cycling community in Ireland. Founded in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic, we realised the mental-health toll the lockdowns and isolation were taking on us. We decided to found this community to meet like-minded cycling enthusiasts. While cycling may be the common element for each of our members, we do far more together than just cycle! Just check out our events page!

Paulo is a Co-Founder of Cycling Everywhere, founding the community for cyclists in Ireland to come together during a challenging time. Cycling has brought us all together during the many lockdowns, getting us all some fresh air and new friends. As the community continues to expand, Paulo continues to plan a variety of cycling routes each month, for every level of difficulty.

Av avid cyclist enthusiast for many years, Jennifer found lockdown to be the perfect time to co-found a community of individuals with similar interests. Cycling as a community allowed for social distancing, while still getting to meet and talk with others. From here the community expanded and we began to plan events outside of our cycling. Cycling was a passion for each of us, but we continue to enjoy many activites outside of cycling.
